FAQs

What are you specialised in?
We are specialised in Candid Photography & Cinematic Wedding film.
What is your way of working ( Photography ) ?
We use minimum two photographers; one who covers the entire traditional part which includes over all rituals and functions coverage, group & family photos etc. Other Photographer covers the Candid part, which includes natural moments, classy portraits. If you want more of crowd coverage we can add an extra photographer.
What is Candid Photography?
Candid Photography is when pictures are taken with some high end cameras, from certain distance, without making you aware, clicking the natural moment, in natural light without flash. For example, if we ask you to smile and click your picture then you will give a conscious smile, pictures will not turn out that good. On other hand if you are having time with your family, friends we try to capture that natural smile.
How many images do you provide? Is there a limitation to it?
There are no limitations as we charge for number of hours we work for and not the number of pictures we click. Approx. We provide 1500-4000 pictures for a day's wedding depending on the functions.
What is Cinematic Wedding film? Do you give full video along with it?
Cinematic film is a short film version of your wedding video that has your perfect moments. We shoot these videos with the cameras which are used to make films. So the quality of the film is professional. Plus, these cameras are capable of shooting from a certain distance, without any extra light so it isn’t fussy like the older times. Here, we use minimum 3 cameras, one that covers the entire wide angle and other two captures the natural moments. As an output you get two videos. 1] Full Length Video of around 1 to 2 hours of every event or function. 2] A cinematic Wedding Film of 2 to 5 mins.
Can you shoot with fewer cameras?
We do not recommend reduction in crew. This multi-camera setup allows us to position ourselves strategically to capture the function more naturally. Reduction of cameras will definitely affect the entire output.
How do you suggest doing a pre-wedding Photo shoot?
Pre-wedding shoots are done in two ways, either a 4 hour small session in Mumbai or a couple of day’s session outside Mumbai. We provide around 300-900 images depending on the time span. We normally do it in natural ambiance; the place for this can be decided mutually.
What about pre-wedding Cinematic Film?
We need minimum 24 hours to shoot a good cinematic pre-wedding film. It is done according to the clients demand within or outside Mumbai. The length of the pre-wedding video will be 1.30-2.30 mins.
What are the common deliverable we can expect?
In standard package, we deliver all processed pictures along with the wedding films (Short and long both). All photos and films are given to you in a hard disk provided by you.
Can we go for only still photography or only cinematic film? Or we have to take both?
While shooting for any event, the photo and video team should have a good co-ordination as it results better. Although we prefer handling both together, we are comfortable taking up either as per client's requirement. But we strongly recommend that there shouldn't be any other team working on the same project.
Do you make coffee table books or Albums?
Yes, we do. One album roughly accommodates 200-220 pictures. Costing for the albums varies according to the quality and number of pictures.
Who picks up the music for the film?
It is a joint decision of the client and the team.
Which all location your team can travel to cover our wedding ?
We are open to travel all across the world.
Are there any other costs, hidden or extra other than the ones mentioned to us in the quotation?
Expenses for Travel, food and accommodation to be borne by you. Other than that, hard disks and pen drives to be provided by you for the final delivery. Albums (if not included in the contract) should be paid separately. Any additional Hours / days of shooting (not mentioned in contract) shall be charged extra. No other hidden costs.
